Not There? Add a new Paper Contact!

MyContacts works best with contacts who are also on the MyContacts platform. As a relatively new platform however, we do realize that not all of your contacts will be on here yet, though they will in the future. Until they get started with their own MyContacts, you can keep track of your contact list by adding what we call a 'Paper Contact'.

A MyContact Paper Contact is a simple and modernized method of storing a contact who is not yet on the MyContacts platform. The name comes from the traditional method of storing contacts, using a paper based system, due to the fact that it lacks some of the more advanced MyContacts features. It's a great way to get started with storing your contacts whom are not yet on the platform, and also an easy way to refer them to come sign up, and be notified once they do.
